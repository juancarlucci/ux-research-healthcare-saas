# HealthFlow Context

## What HealthFlow Is
HealthFlow is a comprehensive practice management platform for acupuncturists and holistic healthcare practitioners. It handles:
- **Scheduling & Calendar Management**
- **Patient Booking Portal**
- **SOAP Notes** (HIPAA compliant)
- **Billing & Insurance**
- **Herbal Dispensary Management**

## Primary Users
1. **Practitioners** (acupuncturists, chiropractors, holistic health professionals)
   - Establishing new practices or running established ones
   - Need: Smooth practice setup, efficient patient management
   
2. **Patients**
   - Booking appointments online
   - Managing their care journey
   - Need: Simple, trustworthy booking experience
